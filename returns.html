Returns & Exchange Policy

Please read our policy carefully before placing your order.

🚫

No Returns or Exchanges

At GnA Ethnic Wears, we do not accept returns or exchanges once an order has been placed and dispatched — unless the item arrives damaged or faulty.

✅

Exception — Damaged or Faulty Items Only

We only accept a return or exchange if your item arrives damaged, torn, or with a manufacturing defect. We take great pride in the quality of our products and take all damage claims seriously.

  • ✓Accepted: Item arrived with a tear, hole, or visible damage
  • ✓Accepted: Item has a manufacturing defect (broken zip, missing button, stitching fault)
  • ✓Accepted: Wrong item sent by us (different product or colour than ordered)
  • ✗Not accepted: Change of mind after ordering
  • ✗Not accepted: Wrong size ordered (please check our size guide before ordering)
  • ✗Not accepted: Colour looks different on screen vs in person (colours may vary slightly due to photography)
  • ✗Not accepted: Item has been worn, washed, or altered
✦ If Your Item Is Damaged

How to Report a Damaged Item

1
Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order

You must report a damaged item within 48 hours of delivery. We cannot accept claims after this period.

2
Send clear photos of the damage

WhatsApp or email us clear photos of the damaged area and the full item. Also include your order details.

3
We review and respond within 24 hours

We will review your photos and confirm whether the damage qualifies. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

4
We send a replacement or offer a refund

If approved, we will send a replacement item or issue a full refund including shipping. The choice is yours.

📏
Avoid Size Issues — Read Before Ordering
Since we do not accept returns for sizing issues, we strongly recommend:
  • Check our Size Guide carefully before ordering
  • When between sizes, always size up — Indian sizing runs slightly small
  • WhatsApp us your measurements and we'll recommend the right size
  • Each product listing shows exactly which sizes are available

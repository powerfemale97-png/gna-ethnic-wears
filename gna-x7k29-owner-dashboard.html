How to publish products to your website
Step 1: Add or edit your products here in the admin panel.
Step 2: Click ⬇️ Export Products File above — this downloads gna-products-data.js.
Step 3: Replace the existing gna-products-data.js in your website folder with the downloaded file.
Step 4: Re-upload to your web host (Netlify / cPanel) — products will appear on all collection pages immediately!

✦ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship worldwide? ▾
Yes! We ship to Australia, New Zealand, USA, UK, Canada, India, Singapore, UAE and all countries worldwide. Shipping is calculated automatically via Australia Post at checkout.
How is shipping calculated? ▾
Shipping is automatically calculated at checkout based on your country and selected shipping method (Standard or Express) via Australia Post rates. Australian orders over AUD $150 get FREE shipping!
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Afterpay, Zip Pay, and Bank Transfer. All payments are processed securely.
What sizes are available? ▾
We stock sizes XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, 3XL, 4XL, 5XL and 6XL. However sizes vary per individual product — not every dress comes in every size. Always check the size availability on each product listing. Visit our Size Guide for full measurements. Need help choosing? WhatsApp or call us on 0494 581 796 — we are available 24/7!
Can I return or exchange an item? ▾
We do not accept returns or exchanges unless the item arrives damaged or faulty. If your item arrives damaged, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos of the damage. We will then arrange a replacement or full refund. For size-related issues, please check our Size Guide carefully before ordering. Read our full Returns Policy for details.
